Research

No research has been compiled, but similar results could be extrapliated from alert re-design.

When to use

Use to indicate a large quotation of text that comes from another source.

What to avoid

Do not use when content needs to stand out but is not a quote or citation. Use wells, alerts (when appropriate), or callouts (proposed) instead.

Implementation

Replaces the current design for the <blockquote> element. Examples below are wrapped in .experimental class in order to not use overriding styles.

Excerpt use

Used to call out quote within a block of text. Used primarily to summarize a block of text with a quote, similar to news articles, or highlight an existing quote in the text using the <q> element.
