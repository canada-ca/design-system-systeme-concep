Research

No research has been compiled, but similar results could be extrapliated from alert re-design.

When to use

Use to indicate a large quotation of text that comes from another source.

What to avoid

Do not use when content needs to stand out but is not a quote or citation. Use wells, alerts (when appropriate), or callouts (proposed) instead.
